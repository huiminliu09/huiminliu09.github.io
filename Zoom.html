Answer Me This- Zoom

Answer Me This -Zoom is a voice-activated search engine that answers your questions without leaving Zoom. Users can receive real time information in their chat bar whether they need a simple conversion for a math assignment or an updated COVID statistic while youâ€™re virtually meeting with your family across the globe.

Motivation:

  • Distraction in navigating between Zoom and Google to search for information
  • Existence of misinformation that prevents fruitful discussion on Zoom meetings
  • Lack of key information related that is useful for the group meeting
  • Interruptions of the flow of meaningful Zoom conversation to ask unrelated or minor questions

Description:

The functional process can be broken down into four parts:

  • Record and convert speech into text
  • Identify Question by using the "Hey Alex" key word as the beginning and then using a pause threshold to interpret the end of the question
  • Query question through the Encyclopedia Britannica and the Google Search APIs
  • Enter the responses in the Zoom chat window
